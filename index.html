ABOUT US

Kids Party Station offers a unique and thrilling gaming experience for children of all ages. Our custom-designed gaming van sets the stage for hours of fun, providing an unparalleled setting for gaming adventures.

Step into our state-of-the-art gaming van, a paradise featuring 8 PlayStation 5 consoles and 8 4K monitors. This setup allows up to 16 kids to simultaneously engage in gaming, making it the pinnacle of gaming party experiences.

From high-speed racing in the latest driving games to embarking on epic adventures, or facing off in multiplayer competitions, our van caters to all gaming preferences.

Each gaming station is equipped with its own high-quality headphones, ensuring that every player is immersed in rich, detailed soundscapes without distraction. Combined with comfortable seating and dynamic lighting, Kids Party Station creates a memorable, personalized atmosphere where kids can immerse themselves in their favorite games alongside friends."
